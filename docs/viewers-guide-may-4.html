MLB
Major League Baseball · Week 6 of 2026
96 games · All Times ET

The biggest baseball week of the US spring. Six straight days of national marquee windows — Apple TV+ Friday, NBC Sunday Night Baseball, plus the second full slate since the 2026 rights realignment. ESPN Unlimited carries midweek games. Netflix dark this week; they are back for the July 4 weekend.

Where to Watch MLB.TV · ESPN Unlimited · NBC/Peacock · Apple TV+ · FOX · TBS/Max MLB.TV ~$149.99 season Full MLB Guide →
Did You Know
MLB games this week stream across seven different US platforms — more than any single week in the sport's history. The 2026 rights realignment means a Monday night game might be on ESPN, Tuesday on TBS, Wednesday on Apple, Friday on Apple again, Saturday on FOX, and Sunday on NBC. This is why a weekly viewer's guide exists.

NPB
Nippon Professional Baseball · Regular Season
~36 games · Times in ET

Pacific League and Central League both running. Pacific League TV carries all 6 PL clubs live for US fans (~$11/month). Central League remains fragmented by team rights and is largely geoblocked — the Giants and Tigers are best accessed during the interleague window starting May 26, when Central teams travel to Pacific parks.

Did You Know
The NPB interleague window — May 26 to June 14 — is the single best window for US fans to see Central League stars all year. During those three weeks, Central League teams (Giants, Tigers, Carp, etc.) travel to Pacific League parks, and those games are carried on the US-available Pacific League TV. This is the three-week window when Yomiuri Giants games become legally streamable in the United States. Mark your calendar.

KBO
Korean Baseball Organization · Regular Season
~30 games · Times in ET

Every KBO game this week streams free worldwide on SOOP. No account required. No geoblock. Six games a day most weekdays, five on weekends. 12.3 million fans in 2025 — a Korean baseball record — and the league is riding that wave into 2026 with no signs of slowing.

Did You Know
KBO cheerleading isn't a half-inning break — it's a full-time career with choreographed chants, drum lines, and organized crowd singing throughout the entire game. Each KBO club employs a dedicated cheer squad and a professional "cheermaster" who leads the stadium through the ninth inning. It's the most intentionally entertaining in-game experience in professional baseball anywhere on Earth. Free on SOOP. Hit play.

Taiwan beat Japan 4-0 at Tokyo Dome to win the 2024 Premier12 — the first time a team not named Japan, Korea, or the United States has won a WBSC premier tournament on home soil like that. Nobody in America noticed. CPBL has been the quiet story of international baseball's 2020s.

LMB
Liga Mexicana de Béisbol · 101st Season
~36 games · Times in ET

Mexico's 101st professional season. 20 teams. Diablos Rojos del México chasing a three-peat after back-to-back championships. Streaming on jonron.tv (paid) with select games on LMB's own YouTube channel. The season opened April 16 and runs through August.

Did You Know
Guadalajara is Mexico's baseball capital and nobody talks about it. The Charros de Jalisco draw consistent 15,000+ crowds at Estadio Panamericano, built for the 2011 Pan American Games. WBN reporters Matt Tallarini and Leif Skodnick have both filed from Guadalajara in 2025 — the Charros-Diablos rivalry is one of the best atmospheres anywhere in baseball, and it is a direct flight from most major US cities.

Italian Baseball League · FIBS · WBN Certified Partner
~8 games · Times in ET

FIBSchannel3 on YouTube carries most games for free, worldwide, with real-time scoring overlays built in partnership with WBSC technology partner Cloud GFX. 2:00 PM ET first pitch = prime afternoon viewing.

Italy's 2026 WBC semifinal run — led by manager Francisco Cervelli — was powered almost entirely by Serie A veterans and Italian-American dual citizens who had come up through this league.
