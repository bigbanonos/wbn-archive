The Fact
Italy's 2026 WBC Run Rewrote the European Baseball Story
At WBC 2026, Team Italy went 5-0 in pool play in Houston, beat the Dominican Republic, and advanced to the semifinals before being eliminated by eventual champion Venezuela. It was the deepest Italian run in WBC history. Manager Francisco Cervelli — the ex-Yankee catcher — built a roster mixing MLB-affiliated prospects of Italian descent with genuine Serie A talent, proving European baseball isn't a novelty. The tournament star was Sam Aldegheri, who threw a complete-game shutout against Brazil. Meanwhile, Serie A winger Renzo Martini was the tournament's best hitter in the European Championship run-up. The combination of a professional league streaming free on YouTube, real-time WBSC-grade statistics on broadcast, and legit MLB-caliber talent has Italy positioned to be Europe's top baseball nation for the rest of this decade. Olympic baseball returns at LA28 — and Italy will be in the mix.
