The Fact
2026 is the Rights Realignment Year — Everything Changed
For 35 years, Sunday Night Baseball lived on ESPN. That's over. Starting 2026, the Sunday game moves to NBC/Peacock. ESPN pivoted to become the retail home for MLB.TV, distributing the out-of-market package that MLB itself owns. Netflix entered the ecosystem with marquee events — Opening Night, the Derby, Field of Dreams, and in Japan all 47 WBC games exclusively. Apple quietly locked down Friday nights, FOX held the World Series, and TBS kept Tuesdays. If you haven't updated your subscriptions since the 2025 season, you're probably paying for the wrong thing.
