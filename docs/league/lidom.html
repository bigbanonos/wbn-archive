⚡ When to Watch LIDOM from the US
Dominican Republic is on Atlantic Standard Time (UTC-4) — same clock as US Eastern most of the year. Weeknight games at 19:30 AST = 7:30 PM ET. Weekend day games at 16:00 AST = 4:00 PM ET. LIDOM is the most US-convenient winter league after LVBP.

The Fact
Every MLB Team's Dominican Academy Feeds LIDOM First
Every one of the 30 MLB organizations operates a baseball academy in the Dominican Republic. Young Dominican prospects — 16-year-olds signed to professional contracts — live, eat, and train at these academies for months before they ever see the United States. But before they can sign with an MLB team, almost all of them play for a LIDOM club first. The academies feed the winter league, and the winter league feeds MLB. Juan Soto, Vladimir Guerrero Jr., Rafael Devers, Fernando Tatis Jr., Julio Rodríguez, Ketel Marte — every single one came up through this system. The town of San Pedro de Macorís alone — home of Estrellas Orientales — has produced more MLB shortstops than any other city on Earth. When you watch LIDOM, you're watching the most productive baseball pipeline in the world, and you're watching the country whose WBC roster made it to the finals four times. This isn't exhibition baseball. This is where the MLB roster for 2030 is being built right now.
