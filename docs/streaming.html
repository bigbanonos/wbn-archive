International & Governing Body Events

WBSC · DAZN · FIBS · ABL
⚡ DAZN × WBSC — The 8-Year Global Deal (Oct. 2024)
DAZN and the WBSC signed an 8-year global distribution partnership making DAZN the primary international distributor for all WBSC premium baseball and softball events. For U.S. fans this is the most significant international baseball streaming deal in years.

DAZN holds exclusive U.S. rights to the Premier12 — the only legal way to watch it in America. For other WBSC World Cups and Olympic Qualifiers, DAZN is the primary U.S. platform, though rights vary by specific tournament. Outside the U.S., Premier12 is on DAZN's free tier in English, Spanish, Japanese, and Korean.
US Exclusive
Premier12 — DAZN only; paid subscription required in US. Free globally outside US.
Also Covered
WBSC Baseball World Cups (U-12, U-15, U-18, U-23, Women's), Olympic Qualifiers, Softball & Baseball5 World Cups
Excluded Territories
Japan, Korea, Taiwan, Mexico — local rights holders only. Spanish-language excluded in most Latin American markets.
How to Access (US)
dazn.com → subscribe. App available on Apple TV, Roku, Fire TV, iOS, Android, smart TV.

MLB — The Major Leagues

30 Teams · 2026 New Rights Era

Commissioner: Rob Manfred · 2026–2028 rights cycle began this season · all deals verified against MLB.com press releases

ESPN Unlimited
Midweek national package (30 exclusive games). Now the retail home for MLB.TV — MLB owns the product, ESPN distributes it. Subscribe and watch out-of-market games via the ESPN app. In-market games blacked out. ~$29.99/mo · espn.com
NBC / Peacock
Sunday Night Baseball — exclusive from 2026, ending ESPN's 35-year run. All Wild Card round games (NBC + NBCSN + Peacock). MLB Sunday Leadoff (18 early-day games). peacocktv.com
Netflix
Exclusive marquee events only (2026–2028): Opening Night (Yankees vs. Giants, March 25 — first-ever live MLB on Netflix), Home Run Derby, Field of Dreams Game, select annual specials. All 47 WBC 2026 games exclusive in Japan. netflix.com
Apple TV+
Friday Night Baseball doubleheaders — exclusive all season. ~$85M/yr rights deal. A single Dodgers-Yankees game drove 722,000 new signups in 2025. ~$12.99/mo · tv.apple.com
FOX / FS1 / FOX One
World Series, LCS, Division Series, All-Star Game, select regular season. All 47 WBC 2026 games in the U.S. FOX One streams every FOX Sports broadcast. foxsports.com
TBS / Max
Tuesday Night Baseball (regular season). LCS and Division Series playoff games. All TBS games stream on Max. max.com
MLB.TV (via ESPN app)
MLB-owned out-of-market product — every game outside your local market (~2,430/yr). Subscribe through ESPN app or MLB app. In-market games blacked out. Record 19.4B minutes watched in 2025. mlb.com/tv
MLB Network
24/7 baseball. Live games (Showcase package), Hot Stove, MLB Tonight. Distributed by ESPN from 2026. mlb.com/network
