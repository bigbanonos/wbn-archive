⚡ The Interleague Window — Best Time for US Fans to Watch NPB
May 26 – June 14: Interleague play is the golden window for international fans. When Central League teams travel to Pacific League parks, those games are streamed on Pacific League TV — meaning you can legally watch Yomiuri Giants, Hanshin Tigers, and other Central League stars that are otherwise blacked out internationally.

Best matchup to watch: Yomiuri Giants vs. SoftBank Hawks — the two most storied franchises in each league, meeting at a PL stadium = on PLTV = streamable in the US.

Note: Giants and Tigers home interleague games remain geoblocked outside Japan. You need them to be the road team at a Pacific League park.
