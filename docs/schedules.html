⚡ The Interleague Window — Best Time for US Fans to Watch NPB
May 26 – June 14: Interleague play is the golden window for international fans. When Central League teams travel to Pacific League parks, those games are streamed on Pacific League TV — meaning you can legally watch Yomiuri Giants, Hanshin Tigers, and other Central League stars that are otherwise blacked out internationally.

Best matchup to watch: Yomiuri Giants vs. SoftBank Hawks — the two most storied franchises in each league, meeting at a PL stadium = on PLTV = streamable in the US.

Note: Giants and Tigers home interleague games remain geoblocked outside Japan. You need them to be the road team at a Pacific League park.

🏆 Serie del Caribe 2027

Feb 2027 · Mexico · Tentative
⚾ The Caribbean Championship — Four Champions, One Title
The Serie del Caribe brings together the champions of the four Caribbean winter leagues — LIDOM (Dominican Republic), LVBP (Venezuela), LAMP (Mexico), and LBPRC (Puerto Rico) — in a single round-robin tournament every February. It is the oldest professional baseball tournament in the world still being played.

2027 Location: Mexico (Mexicali and Hermosillo — tentative). Expect massive crowds. The Caribbean Series in Mexico draws some of the largest baseball crowds outside of MLB and Japan.
Format
Round Robin (all 4 teams) → Semifinals → Final. 7–9 days total.
Dates
Early February 2027. Official announcement expected November 2026.
Teams
Champions of LIDOM · LVBP · LAMP · LBPRC
How to Watch
YouTube — free international broadcast. Search "Serie del Caribe 2027" when the event begins.
🕐 Game times: Mexico host games typically 20:00–21:00 NYC (MST = NYC −2hrs, CST = NYC −1hr). All times depend on final venue confirmation.
⚠️ Note on 2025–26: Venezuela (Magallanes) did NOT participate in the 2026 Caribbean Series for the first time since 2003 — relocated to Mexico due to geopolitical tensions. Venezuela hosted the 2026 Serie de las Américas instead. Normal Caribbean Series structure resumes for 2027.

4th Edition · First with 16 Teams · Road to LA28 Olympics
The 2027 WBSC Premier12 is the flagship international baseball tournament — expanded for the first time to 16 teams (from 12). COVID delayed the expansion from 2023; it's finally happening. Chinese Taipei are defending champions, having defeated Japan 4–0 in the 2024 final. The tournament also serves as an Olympic qualifier for LA28 — top team from Asia and top team from Europe/Oceania earn spots.
QUALIFYING — 2026
Oct 2026 → Barcelona, Spain (Anella Olímpica)
Nov 2026 → Zhongshan, China
TOURNAMENT — 2027
Dates and host cities TBD. Groups announced April 2027.
FORMAT
Opening Round (4 groups of 4) → Second Round (2 groups of 4) → Super Round (4 teams) → Gold Medal Game
OLYMPIC STAKES
Top Asia team + Top Europe/Oceania team → automatic LA28 Olympic berths
12 Qualified Teams (by Dec 31, 2025 rankings):
Asia: Japan (1st) · Chinese Taipei (2nd) · South Korea (4th)  |  Americas: USA · Venezuela · Mexico · Puerto Rico · Panama · Cuba · Dominican Republic  |  Europe: Netherlands  |  Oceania: Australia

4 additional teams qualify via 2026 tournaments in Barcelona and Zhongshan. Italy (14th) and Czech Republic (15th) are Europe's main qualifier contenders.
